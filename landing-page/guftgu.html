What makes us different

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built from the ground up for India — with privacy, language, and real human connection at the core.

🎭

Absolute Anonymity

No phone number. No email. No real name. We generate a random 7-digit Guftgu number just for you. Your identity stays 100% private — even we don't know who you are. Share your thoughts, secrets, and feelings without fear of judgment.

📞

WebRTC Voice Calls

Crystal clear, peer-to-peer voice calls with zero server recording. Echo cancellation and noise suppression built-in. Your conversations stay between you and the other person.

🎯

Smart Matching

Match by mood, language, and region. Feeling lonely? Happy? Curious? We find someone who gets it. Priority matching — language + region first, then language only, then region.

📱

Dial by Guftgu Number

Call your friends directly using their 7-digit Guftgu number. No need to share your real number. Stay anonymous even with people you trust.

💬

Friends & Text Chat

Send friend requests during calls. Once connected, chat via end-to-end messaging. Rename friends with nicknames only you can see. Block, unfriend — you're in control.

🐱

30+ SVG Avatars

Animals, people, fantasy characters — pick one that represents you without revealing your face. Animals 🦊, Professions 👩‍⚕️, Fantasy 🧙 — all beautifully crafted.

Built-in protection

Your safety,
our promise

Every feature is designed to keep you safe while staying completely free to express yourself.

🚫
One-tap Block
Block anyone instantly during a call or from your friends list. Blocked users can't match with you, call you, or send requests.
⚠️
Report System
Report inappropriate behavior directly from the match screen or call. Every report is reviewed seriously.
🔒
Friends-Only Chat
Text messaging is locked to mutual friends only — both sides must agree before any messages can be sent.
🗑️
Delete Everything
Delete your account and all data — profile, history, friends, conversations — completely wiped with 30-day recovery window.
