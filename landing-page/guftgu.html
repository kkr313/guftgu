Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Guftgu really anonymous?
▼

Yes! Guftgu requires no phone number, email, or real name. You get a random 7-digit number and a fun avatar. Even we don't know who you are. Your privacy is our #1 priority.

Is Guftgu free to use?
▼

Absolutely. Guftgu is 100% free — no subscriptions, no hidden charges, no premium tiers. Just open the app in your browser and start talking.

What languages does Guftgu support?
▼

Guftgu supports 9+ Indian languages: Hindi, English, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Kannada, Punjabi, Odia, and Bhojpuri. We're adding more!

Do I need to download an app?
▼

No download needed! Guftgu is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works directly in your browser — mobile or desktop. You can also install it to your home screen for an app-like experience.

How does matching work?
▼

Our smart matching considers your language, mood, and region preferences. It first tries an exact match, then relaxes criteria to find you the best conversation partner — usually in under 30 seconds.

Can I call someone directly?
▼

Yes! Once you know someone's 7-digit Guftgu number, you can dial them directly from the app — just like a real phone call, but fully anonymous.
